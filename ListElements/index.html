Jaya's Recipe for the Best White Sauce Vegan Pasta

Ingredients

For the White Sauce:

  • 12 Cashews
  • 5 Black Pepper balls

For the dish:

  • 2 cups Uncooked Pasta
  • 10 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • 6 Green Chillies
  • 1 cup Green Peas
  • 1 Carrot
  • Butter
  • Pink salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in a pan with 2 cups water and a pinch of salt.
  2. Soak the cashew for 20 mins in 1/4 cup water.
  3. Chop garlic, onion, and green chilies into small cubes.
  4. Cut carrots into cubes.
  5. Add cashews with some water and black pepper into a blender and make a smooth paste.
  6. Take a pan and add 1 tsp butter. Let it heat. Add garlic to it. Roast until golden.
  7. Add green chilies and onion and roast them. Now add veggies and saute.
  8. Add salt and let the veggies cook.
  9. Now, add cooked pasta to the pan.
  10. Add cashew sauce to the pan.
  11. Let them all mix, and turn off the stove.
  12. Tasty pasta is ready !!
